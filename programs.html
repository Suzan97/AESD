Our Programmes

AESD is dedicated to providing a wide range of programs and services designed to meet the needs of the community. From educational initiatives and skill-building workshops on environmental conservation and sustainable livelihoods, AESD strives to empower individuals and foster a supportive environment for growth and development. AESD remains committed to enhancing the quality of lives in Kitui, Taita Taveta and Kajiado.

Sustainable Waste Management

AESD works towards reduction of the amount of municipal waste that is produced in urban areas through employing strategies of waste handling, and dumping in landfills. AESD programmes works towards waste accumulation controls through:

  • Composting
  • Pyrolysis
  • Recycling
  • Capacity building of households and industry on waste handling and disposal.

Child and Adolescent Protection

AESD is committed to protecting children and adolescents from abuse, exploitation, and neglect. Our programs focus on:

  • Advocacy and awareness campaigns
  • Support services for affected children
  • Training and capacity building for caregivers

Water for Sustainable Livelihoods

Water is the backbone of community livelihoods. We contribute to enhanced water resources management and investment in the three counties – for improved and sustained water access for households’ sanitation, hygiene and their productive needs.

  • Boreholes drilling
  • Water pans rehabilitation and excavation
  • Water sources management

Terrestrial Ecosystems and Sustainable Agriculture

We build community resilience to climate change – especially drastic climate conditions that impact negatively on community livelihoods (i.e. drought and floods). Explore the key mitigation measures outlined below, designed to proactively address challenges, reduce risks, and foster sustainable solutions for a safer and more resilient future.

  • Afforestation and agro-ecology
  • Propagation of seedlings and distribution to community
  • Green sustainable agriculture
  • Agro-ecology
  • Irrigation-fed agriculture

Public Health Promotions and Emergency Responses

AESD promotes public health and provides emergency responses to ensure the well-being of communities. Our initiatives include:

  • Health education and awareness campaigns
  • Emergency medical services
  • Support for health infrastructure
