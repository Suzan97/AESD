Empowering Communities through Sustainable Agriculture

In 2024, AESD launched a groundbreaking project in the rural areas of Kenya aimed at empowering local communities through sustainable agriculture. The project focused on training farmers in modern agricultural techniques, providing them with high-quality seeds, and introducing innovative irrigation systems.

As a result, over 500 farmers have seen a significant increase in their crop yields, leading to improved food security and economic stability in the region. The project also emphasized the importance of environmental conservation, teaching farmers how to maintain soil health and reduce their carbon footprint.
